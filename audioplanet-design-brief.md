# Audio Planet — Website Redesign Brief
### For use with Stitch / Antigravity

---

## 1. Business Overview

**Client:** Audio Planet (`audioplanet.co.in`)
**Location:** Bangalore, India
**Founded:** 1999
**Industry:** Premium Hi-Fi Audio Consulting, Home Theater Systems, Acoustic Design

Audio Planet is not a regular electronics store. They are Bangalore's premier audio consultancy — a destination for audiophiles, architects, interior designers, and high-net-worth individuals seeking museum-grade home listening experiences. They are an authorized dealer of the world's finest audio brands and offer end-to-end services from acoustic room design to system integration and after-sales care.

Their business sits at the intersection of **luxury, expertise, and craft** — comparable to how a bespoke tailor operates vs. a clothing retailer.

## 4. Visual Direction

### Aesthetic: **Dark Luxury / Acoustic Minimalism**

Think: the inside of a high-end recording studio meets a luxury showroom. Deep, rich darks. Warm amber and bronze accents that evoke analog warmth. Generous negative space. Typography that commands attention. The feel of holding a Naim Audio product — precise, weighted, purposeful.

### Color Palette

```
Background (primary):   #0A0A0A — near-black, like anechoic foam
Surface (cards/panels): #111111 / #181818
Accent (gold/amber):    #C9A84C — warm analog gold
Accent (glow):          #E8C97A — highlight, hover states
Text (primary):         #F0EDE6 — warm off-white, not harsh
Text (secondary):       #8A8680 — muted warm gray
Border/divider:         #2A2520 — barely visible, warm dark
```

No purple gradients. No tech-blue. No cold grays.

### Typography

| Role            | Font                                         |
|-----------------|----------------------------------------------|
| Display / Hero  | **Cormorant Garamond** (italic for elegance) |
| Headlines       | **Libre Baskerville** or **Playfair Display** |
| Body            | **DM Sans** (clean, readable, modern)        |
| Labels/Tags     | **Geist Mono** or **Space Mono** (technical precision) |

Type scale should feel editorial — large, confident display sizes. Body text at 17–18px minimum.

### Visual Language

- **Waveform as motif** — subtle SVG audio waveforms as decorative dividers and section transitions
- **Framed images** — product and room photography displayed in thick, bordered "exhibition frames"
- **Generous whitespace** (darkspace) — sections breathe, nothing feels cluttered
- **Warm grain texture** — subtle noise overlay on hero sections for analog depth
- **Gold hairlines** — 1px gold/amber lines as section separators and UI accents
- **No gradients except warm amber glows** — avoid trendy purple/blue gradient clichés

## 8. Interactions & Motion Notes

- **Hero entrance**: Headline fades and slides up on load with staggered sub-text reveal
- **Section entrance**: Each section animates in on scroll (subtle fade + upward translate)
- **Product tiles**: Slight scale-up and amber border glow on hover
- **Nav CTA**: Amber fill button with warm pulse/glow on hover
- **Testimonials**: Smooth fade transition between quotes
- **Partner logos**: Grayscale → color transition on hover
- **Cursor**: Custom cursor (filled circle + ring) for desktop — optional but premium feel
- **Waveform divider**: Animated SVG waveform that pulses gently between sections

---

## 9. What to Avoid

- ❌ Generic electronics store look (think Croma, Reliance Digital)
- ❌ Blue/purple tech gradients
- ❌ White backgrounds — this is a dark-luxury site
- ❌ Cookie-cutter card grids with no visual personality
- ❌ Cheap stock photography — no headphones-on-white-background clichés
- ❌ Cluttered navigation (current site has "Audio System" listed twice in nav — do not repeat this)
- ❌ Slow, heavy image carousels as the hero (current site problem)
- ❌ Sans-serif-only type — this brand needs the warmth of serif display fonts

## 11. Technical Notes

- **Framework preference:** Next.js (or Webflow for no-code delivery)
- **Fonts:** Google Fonts or Adobe Fonts (Cormorant Garamond, Playfair Display, DM Sans)
- **Responsive:** Mobile-first — especially for the product and consulting pages
- **Performance:** Lazy-load images, no heavy carousels
- **SEO-ready:** Proper heading hierarchy, meta descriptions per page
- **Enquiry form:** Lightweight, no full e-commerce needed — this is a consultative business
